## 6. Risk Analysis & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Big Tech Competition
**Threat**: GitHub/Microsoft adds better testing to Copilot  
**Probability**: Medium (50%)  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Move fast, establish brand before they do
- Go deeper (specialized) vs. broader
- Build integrations they can't (open source)
- Potential acqui-hire ($50-200M exit)

### Risk 2: AI Model Costs
**Threat**: GPT-4 API costs eat margins  
**Probability**: Low (costs dropping 90%/year)  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Use smaller models for simple tasks
- Fine-tune custom models (cheaper)
- Cache common patterns
- Usage-based pricing covers costs

### Risk 3: Product Complexity
**Threat**: Testing is hard, AI might not be good enough  
**Probability**: Low (early tests show promise)  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Start simple (unit tests only)
- Human-in-loop for review
- Iterate with user feedback
- Set expectations (AI-assisted, not fully autonomous)

### Risk 4: Developer Skepticism
**Threat**: "AI-generated tests aren't trustworthy"  
**Probability**: Medium (30%)  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Transparency (show how tests are generated)
- Explainability (why these tests)
- Metrics (show coverage improvement)
- Social proof (testimonials from trusted devs)

### Risk 5: Slow Enterprise Adoption
**Threat**: Security concerns delay procurement  
**Probability**: Medium (40%)  
**Impact**: Low (have SMB/individual revenue)  
**Mitigation**:
- SOC 2 compliance from day 1
- Self-hosted option
- Clear data policies (code never stored)
- Case studies from early adopters

**Overall Risk Level**: **Low to Medium** - manageable with good execution
