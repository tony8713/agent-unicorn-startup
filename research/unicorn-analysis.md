## 1. Common Business Models

### SaaS (Software as a Service) - 32%
**Examples**: Stripe, Canva, UiPath, Databricks, Figma
- **Advantages**: Predictable revenue, high margins (70-80%), global scalability
- **Time to Unicorn**: 5-7 years average
- **Key Metrics**: $100M ARR minimum, NRR >120%, CAC payback <12 months
- **Why it works for AI**: Fully automatable, no physical operations

### Marketplace / Platform - 28%
**Examples**: Airbnb, DoorDash, Instacart, Grab, Faire
- **Advantages**: Network effects, capital-light, winner-takes-most dynamics
- **Time to Unicorn**: 6-8 years average
- **Key Metrics**: GMV >$1B, take rate 15-25%, retention >60%
- **AI Opportunity**: Automated matching, pricing, and operations

### Fintech - 18%
**Examples**: Stripe, Klarna, Revolut, Chime, Nubank
- **Advantages**: Massive TAM, sticky products, transaction revenue
- **Time to Unicorn**: 5-7 years average
- **Key Metrics**: Millions of users, >$100M revenue, strong unit economics
- **Regulatory Challenge**: Compliance overhead (manageable with AI)

### Enterprise Software - 12%
**Examples**: Databricks, Snowflake, UiPath, HashiCorp
- **Advantages**: High ACV, strong retention, expansion revenue
- **Time to Unicorn**: 7-9 years average
- **Key Metrics**: >$100M ARR, NRR >130%, logos from F500

### Consumer / Social - 10%
**Examples**: Discord, ByteDance, Epic Games
- **Advantages**: Viral growth, massive scale potential
- **Time to Unicorn**: 3-6 years (highly variable)
- **Key Metrics**: 100M+ users, monetization >$5/user/year
- **Risk**: Winner-takes-all, high failure rate
