# Agent-Built Unicorn Startup Roadmap
## Mission: Build the first $1B+ company entirely created and operated by AI agents

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Market Research (Week 1-2)

### Research & Validation
- [ ] Analyze top 100 unicorns to identify successful patterns
- [ ] Scan emerging markets and technology trends
- [ ] Identify high-margin, scalable business models
- [ ] Research regulatory requirements for AI-operated businesses
- [ ] Analyze venture capital investment trends
- [ ] Conduct competitive analysis in target markets

### Market Selection
- [ ] Score potential markets on: TAM, growth rate, automation potential, margins
- [ ] Select top 3 market opportunities
- [ ] Deep dive on target customer pain points
- [ ] Validate market demand with data scraping and analysis

---

## Phase 2: Business Model & Strategy (Week 3-4)

### Business Planning
- [ ] Define value proposition and unique selling points
- [ ] Create revenue model (SaaS, marketplace, transaction fees, etc.)
- [ ] Design pricing strategy based on market analysis
- [ ] Build financial projections (5-year model)
- [ ] Define key metrics and KPIs

### Legal & Compliance
- [ ] Research business entity structure options
- [ ] Identify AI-specific legal considerations
- [ ] Draft terms of service and privacy policy
- [ ] Research IP protection strategy
- [ ] Plan compliance framework (GDPR, SOC2, etc.)
